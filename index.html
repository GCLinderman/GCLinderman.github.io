About me:

I am an MD PhD student at Yale University, and I have completed my PhD in Applied Mathematics. Broadly, I am interested in developing mathematical and computational solutions to biomedical problems.

Software

I maintain the following software packages:
  • FIt-SNE: Fast Fourier Transform-accelerated Interpolation-based t-SNE
  • ALRA: Adaptively-thresholded Low Rank Approximation for imputation of scRNA-seq data
  • t-SNE Heatmaps: 1D t-SNE heatmaps to visualize expression patterns of hundreds of genes simultaneously in scRNA-seq
  • oocPCA: Out-of-Core Randomized Principal Component Analysis
  • pcafast: Randomized Principal Component Analysis
